1. Trigger the event by clicking on the screen in any valid location.
2. When the screen is clicked, 4 cauldrons are created near each other in embedded in Trees and 3 witches are created in  spots around the spawned Trees. BadApples will also drop from the Trees at a random rate.
3. BlobMonsters will seek out any Miner and eat them until no Miners are left. 
4. The new entity is the Witch, these Witches will seek out BadApples and once full(3 BadApples) will return to the cauldrons to turn them into a potion. The potion will not be a created entity but the Witches will carry the potion on them and use it to turn any OreBlob into a BlobMonster.
